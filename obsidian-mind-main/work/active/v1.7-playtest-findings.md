23:34). Findings below were measured at `e8625f3`; none of them touch the
files the B3 respin is editing, so they should hold — but the §2 numbers are

## 2. Confirmed bugs (objective, measured)

Ordered by player impact, not effort.

### 2.1 Shotgun fires 1 effective pellet out of 5 — pellet fratricide
One shot spawns 5 pellets **co-located** at the muzzle point; measured in an
open field: 5 spawned same-frame, **1 alive two frames later**. Pellets
raycast-hit *each other*: `PlayerBullet` has `collider='box'`, each pellet's
swept ray ignores only `[self, self.player]`, and a raycast hit kills the bullet
regardless of `can_hit` (damage is gated, dying is not). The shotgun has shipped
at ~1/5 damage since v1.5. Mechanism detail + siblings in
[[v1.7-collision-audit]] §F1.

### 2.2 Player spawns half-buried and can barely move until first jump
At spawn the player rests at **y = 0.5** — feet at the ground plane's *centre*
(top is y = 0.5) — and ground speed measures **0.4 u/s vs the tuned 8.0**
(the feet-level swept ray starts inside the ground and reports every direction
blocked). One jump fixes it permanently. Reproduced in all three runs. Likely
`FirstPersonController.__init__`'s own ground snap placing the entity *origin*
(not the feet) at ground top; the custom ground-snap only ever lifts, never
settles (see 2.3).

### 2.3 Ground contact is sloppy: landings rest up to ~0.6u above the floor
After a jump the player rests at **y = 1.594** (feet ~0.59 above the floor) and
stays there — `grounded` is true whenever the down-ray (origin `y−0.1`, length 1)
hits, so any height within ~1.1u of the floor is "standing", and the snap only
corrects upward. Standing height therefore varies per landing (buried at spawn,
floating after falls) — inconsistent eye height, headbob baseline, and blob
shadow gap.

### 2.4 The jump is double-driven — the tuned physics jump never runs as designed
`Player.input('space')` sets `vertical_speed = 18` **and** falls through
`super().input(key)` into `FirstPersonController.jump()`, which drives `y` with
its own `animate_y` tween. The two systems fight; measured apex **1.8u in 0.27s**
vs the physics design's **3.6u in 0.4s**. The tuned `jump_force`/`gravity` knobs
are effectively dead. Direct playability consequence: the tower's hop chain and
z=12 wall were designed against the 3.6u jump; the wall IS still clearable from
the top step (measured), but margins are half of what the numbers say.

### 2.5 Fast enemy presets are silently clamped to 1 shot/second
`aggressive` promises a 0.4s cadence, `patrol_then_attack` 0.8s — but
`Enemy.shoot()` keeps its own `can_shoot` gate at `ENEMY_SHOOT_COOLDOWN = 1.0`,
so any preset cadence below 1.0s is clamped. Measured: aggressive fired **2
shots in 3s** (both hit, 25 each). `AttackNode`'s docstring even notes the
lockstep assumption "with matching durations" — the fast presets don't match.
`cautious` (3.0s) and `default` (1.0s) are unaffected.

### 2.6 Point-blank shots skip thin cover
Bullets spawn at `gun.world_position + camera.forward` (~1.3u ahead of the
eye). Standing flush against the armory's 0.5u wall and firing into it: the
bullet spawned at **x = −9.32, past the wall's far face at −8.0** — the shot
passes through cover it should hit. Any wall ≲1u thick can be shot through at
zero range. ([[v1.7-collision-audit]] §F3.)

### 2.7 The shipped `level.json` has diverged from the curated `levels/v1.json`
Beyond the known reformat + the intended `light` entry
([[v1.7-fix-backlog]] item 3), the shipped file has real content damage:
- **Four marker textures blanked to `""`** — the orange shotgun-pickup markers,
  the green door plate, the blue rifle markers, and the **red lava floor**
  (`texture_red_test.png`) all render as flat untextured colour. Pickup spots
  read as bare white slivers in-game (visible in the spawn screenshot). Root
  cause found on the editor side — an editor open+save cycle strips project
  textures ([[v1.7-editor-ux-audit]] §D4).
- **Stray debris blocks**: a block with `model: assets/models/Shotgun_1.obj` at
  `(3,2,6)` — a giant dark shotgun floating directly in the player's face at
  spawn — plus a white cube at `(3,2,7)`, a colour-test cube at the origin, and
  a tan-tiled cube at `(0,0,-1)`.
- Float noise (`1.2999999523162842`) from editor round-trips.

The curated `levels/v1.json` is intact. Decision needed: restore `level.json`
from it (re-adding the `light` entry), or re-save deliberately after the editor
texture bug is fixed.

### 2.8 Enemies visibly track the player through walls
`Enemy.update()` runs `look_at(player)` gated only on `distance ≤ 100` — not on
detection/LOS. Measured on a patroller with the player fully occluded: body
rotation 244° (= bearing to player) while walking heading 273°. Every enemy on
the map crab-walks its patrol while staring at you through walls — reads broken
and leaks player position information. The v1.7 `DetectPlayerNode` LOS gate
fixed *engagement* but not *facing*.

### 2.9 One unexplained session reset (observed once)
In probe 1, after the player was killed by a chasing enemy mid-run, the game
returned to the **main menu** with no R press (session log shows the full
`return_to_menu` → `main_menu` teardown pair firing from inside the frame
loop). Not reproduced in probes 2–3 (instrumented with a stack-trace hook —
`tools/playtest_probe3.py` keeps it). If a mid-death menu return shows up in
hands-on play, run probe 3 and read `rtm_trace.txt`.

## 3. Verified working (worth knowing what's solid)

- **Boot → intro → menu → game → WIN/GAME_OVER → R → menu → replay**: clean
  across 3 sessions; scene entity count stable at 72 after every menu return
  (no teardown leaks); `respawn_point` correctly reset between sessions.
- **World-edge fall** → global kill plane → GAME OVER (pre-checkpoint terminal) ✓.
- **Checkpoint trigger** sets `respawn_point` on crossing ✓.
- **Armory door**: plate trigger slides the door up exactly 3.0u, stays open ✓.
- **Weapon pickups/inventory**: shotgun and rifle granted to correct slots,
  switching (`1/2/3`) works, ammo HUD tracks (`INF` / `8/8` / `14/24` /
  `RELOADING` all observed correct), reload completes on the scaled clock,
  ammo pickup tops up ✓.
- **Rifle auto-fire** kills reliably; **pistol** 4 shots = kill at 100hp ✓.
- **flee_when_low**: fights at full HP, opens +3.2u distance within 1.5s of
  dropping under the threshold ✓. **patrol** walks its route when unobserved
  (12.7u span in 5s) and chases on detection ✓. **cautious** holds the slow
  cadence ✓ (measured 4 shots/7s vs default's ~7).
- **Enemy fire is lethal**: an AFK player dies to a stalking enemy — probe 2
  proved it accidentally. (Probe 1's "4 shots, no damage" was cover geometry
  between them, not a bug.)
- **Pause mid-firefight** engages/resumes cleanly incl. the deferred
  ThemedButton timing; HUD hides/shows correctly.
- **Frame budget**: median 16.64ms / p95 17.41ms during live play — **zero
  dropped frames at the 60Hz cap** with lit shader + PCF shadows + bloom + a
  firefight. Caveat: vsync-bound measurement (the harness didn't set
  `sync-video 0` — the exact [[brain/Gotchas]] trap), so this proves "holds
  60fps", **not** how much headroom remains.

## 5. Feel observations (subjective — input for Ivan, not findings)

- **Visual stack composes well.** Sun shading + PCF shadows + blob shadows +
  bloom read coherently in motion; the drone's rim/Half-Lambert separates it
  from geometry; shadows anchor the blocks. Screenshots: spawn view, door
  sequence, yard.
- **South faces go near-black.** With ambient 0.06 the unlit faces of grey
  blocks read almost black at some angles (spawn cover blocks, armory).
  Ivan tuned these values live (`da8389f`) — flagging only because the
  screenshots show it strongly; the TF2 "shadowed ≠ black" goal is a judgment
  call.
- **Crosshair** (0.02 scale, tinted) is a faint dot — easy to lose against sky.
- **Menu title text** washes out against the bright sky behind it.
- **Headbob** measured ±0.44u of camera height at frequency 4.5 — strong;
  deliberately tuned (`71e9feb`), so left alone, but worth one hands-on look
  at the same time as the 2.3 baseline drift.
- **Enemy bullets at speed 10** are very dodgeable at range; combined with 2.5
  the intended threat gradient between presets is mostly flattened to "1
  shot/s from everyone". Worth revisiting after 2.5.
- **Patrollers aggro from spawn distance**: the NE yard is ~57u from spawn with
  open sightlines, inside their 60u detection — they start converging early.
  LOS gating works; it's the open map that makes it moot. Level-design call.
